About the Procurement Office

The Procurement Office of Western Mindanao State University (WMSU) in Zamboanga City is responsible for managing and supervising the acquisition of goods, supplies, equipment, and services needed by the university. The office ensures that all procurement processes follow proper government procedures, transparency, accountability, and efficiency in serving the needs of the institution.

This Procurement Management System was developed to help simplify and modernize the procurement process within the university. The system allows departments and colleges to submit requests digitally, monitor procurement activities, and improve communication between the Procurement Office and university stakeholders.
